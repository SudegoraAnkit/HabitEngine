# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 27, 2026

**HabitEngine** (the "Application") is developed as a 100% offline-first, privacy-focused productivity and habit-tracking tool. We believe in absolute data privacy and transparency.

Because the Application is designed to run entirely locally on your device, our data policy is simple: **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information or usage data.**

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

- **No Personal Data Collection:** The Application does not require any account creation, email login, or personal profile setup. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
- **Local Data Storage:** All data you input—including your habit loops (cues, actions, rewards), completion logs, consistency streak data, and activity logs—is stored strictly on your local device. The Application uses a local SQLite database (via Android Room) which resides in secure private app storage on your device.
- **No Remote Transmission:** We do not host or operate any remote databases or servers. None of your data is sent, uploaded, or transmitted over the internet to us or any third parties.

---

## 2. Permissions

The Application may request device storage permissions solely for the following user-initiated features:
- **Exporting Backups:** To write your exported backup files (in JSON or CSV format) to a directory of your choice on your device.
- **Importing Backups:** To read a previously saved backup file to restore your local data.

These actions are entirely under your control, and the Application does not access any other files on your device.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

- **No Analytics or Telemetry:** We do not use any third-party analytics software, crash-reporting SDKs, or performance-monitoring tools that send tracking identifiers or telemetry to external servers.
- **No Advertising:** The Application is completely ad-free and contains no tracking libraries or advertising SDKs.
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** Since we do not collect any information, it is impossible for us to share, sell, or rent your data to third-party services, marketers, or authorities.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- **You Control Your Data:** All local database records reside on your device. You can erase all data by clearing the Application's storage in your Android system settings or by uninstalling the Application. 
- **Backups:** Any backups you export (JSON/CSV) are files stored on your local disk or cloud drive. You can delete these backup files at any time using your device's file manager.

---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by posting the updated Privacy Policy in the Application's repository and built-in FAQ section. Since we do not collect user contact info, we recommend checking this document periodically for any updates.
